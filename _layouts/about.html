About Me

Hi there! I am Julia Liu, from Bay Area, California. I am an undergraduate student at University of California, Berkeley, Class of 2022, fulfilling my B.A. in Computer Science and B.S. in Business Administration. I am also passionate about exploring the entrepreneurial side of tech and innovation and will thus also be completing my Certificate in Entrepreneurship & Technology at Cal.

I have been designing websites since 2012 and love building full-stack web and mobile apps in my free time. I am also taking AI/ML/NLP courses at Berkeley and am constantly looking for more ways to involve myself in the tech space.

  • I was a STEP Intern at Google for Summer 2020.
  • I am a TA for a web design decal course at Berkeley.
  • I created and released multiple web and iOS apps, including Verze, Dreamemo, and Kwizu.

Since participating in FBLA in high school, I became involved in various management, consulting, and entrepreneurship organizations as well, both in and outside campus.

  • I am currently a Growth Equity Intern at Greycroft.
  • I am the VP of Marketing for DiversaTech, where I was previously a strategy consultant and project manager for a data analysis project.
  • I was also VP of Program Operations for the UC Launch Startup Accelerator for the 2020 cohort.
  • I worked as a Management Intern for Win-Kel, an "Airbnb for storage" startup summer 2019.
  • I was the VP of Innovation for AccelerateHer, where I led the spring 2020 fellowship program for femxle-focused startup teams.
  • I was a college ambassador for Mochi Magazine, an Asian-American-focused media platform.

Besides coding away hours of my life, living off the caffeine of my endless milk tea and yerba mate drinks, I also love spending time indulging in my hobbies. You can catch me traveling (watch my Youtube vlogs!), lurking on Twitter, writing unpublished stories, or practicing my awkward dancing skills on dance teams.
